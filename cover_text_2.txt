2. The Lighthouse
Standing tall and proud against the relentless pounding of the waves, the lighthouse cast its watchful gaze over the endless expanse of the ocean, a solitary sentinel(italic). White paint, once pristine, was now streaked with grey, a testament to the salty air and storms it had weathered. A lone seagull perched on the balcony, its piercing cry echoing through the crisp morning air.  Inside(underline), a dedicated keeper meticulously charted the course of passing ships, their blinking lights a fleeting dance against the inky darkness.
